Hey, I’m Raine. I’m a lead technician at uBreakiFix, where I spend my days fixing technology, troubleshooting weird repair issues, and keeping hardware—both old and new—running like a dream. Whether it’s reviving vintage computers, optimizing a Linux server, or getting a modern phone back in working order, I love making tech work the way it’s supposed to.

I live at the intersection of retro and modern. One minute, I’m running Windows XP on a CRT; the next, I’m SSH-ing into my Linux server to manage self-hosted cloud storage. I love the simplicity and intentionality of older tech but also appreciate the power and flexibility of modern systems. To me, the best setup is one that blends the two seamlessly.

Beyond tech, I’m into AI, transhumanism, and nature. I have an AAS in Fish & Wildlife Technology, and I believe in a future where technology and humanity evolve together in a way that benefits all life.

When I’m not repairing devices or tweaking my own setups, I’m probably playing an RPG, watching Scrubs with my girlfriend, or getting way too into golf—even if it's just in video game form (for now).

This site is where I share my projects, thoughts, and whatever else sparks my interest. If you’re into tech repair, Linux, retro gaming, or just geeking out over cool hardware, you’re in the right place.
